Maintenance after playing

Removing moisture in flute body

  1. Pass the cloth through the whole at the to of the cleaning rod
  2. Wrap the cleaning rod using the cloth
  3. Push the rod through the flute and remove the moisture by turning it the same direction as how the cloth is wrapped

Removing moisture from the pads

When the pad is moist after playing, use the cleaning paper or the cigarette paper to remove the moisture.


place the cleaning paper below the key and gently press it a few times


Remove the paper when the pad is dry


*Never pull the cleaning paper out while the keys are pressed down*

Removing fingerprints(dirts) from the surface

Use the polishing cloth wipe the surface of the flute every end of the practice.

If there is a black spot formed on the flute, use the silver polish to get rid of it
